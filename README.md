## 📌 About

**Comment allez-vous?** is a platform where people share their feelings, their days, or whatever is on their minds.

No pressure. No algorithm pretending to understand you. No dopamine casino disguised as social media.

---

## 💬 Philosophy

- No likes  
- No comments  
- No validation system  
- Just posts that exist, quietly, without performance metrics attached  

Because not everything needs to become a popularity contest.

---

## 🚀 Why this exists

Most platforms turn expression into entertainment.

This one doesn’t.

You write. It stays. That’s it.
